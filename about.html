Shawn Musgrave is a Boston-based investigative reporter whose work has appeared in the Boston Globe, Politico, VICE, Motherboard, The Marshall Project, and many other publications. He first began researching the Massachusetts drug lab scandals in 2015 while at the New England Center for Investigative Reporting.

In 2017, he received generous funding from the Fund for Investigative Journalism to continue his research.
